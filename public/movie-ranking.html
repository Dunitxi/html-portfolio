The Best Civil War Movies According to Zack

My top 3 Civil War movies of all-tine

Gods & Generals

A masterpiece showing the first half of the American Civil War. The movie also focuses on the last days of Stonewall Jacksons.

Gettysburg

The sequel to Gods & Generals. Based on the book "The Killer Angels". Gives an account of the battle that turned the tide of the American Civil War.

Glory

Follows the story of one of the first Black Union regiments during the American Civil War.
